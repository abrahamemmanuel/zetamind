Sales

The sales module manages sales quotations, orders, price lists, etc. In Odoo all the modules are linked to each other and if you make a change in one module, it will automatically be updated in the other modules also, which will make your jobs easier.

CRM

Odoo CRM is the Customer Relationship Management module within the Odoo platform. It allows you to see all your leads/opportunities in one location, manage them from one stage to another, and analyze your results.

Inventory

Get the most efficient storage method and improve all your internal actions. Odoo's double inventory has no inventory input, output or transformation. Instead, all operations are inventory movements between locations. Use replenishment rules and automate requests for quotations from your supplier.
